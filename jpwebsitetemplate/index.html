Motivated

From the time I woke up to the time I lay down I am driven and motivated to make something, anything important and productive happen. I enjoy the challenge that comes accomplishments and I enjoy assisting others in their challenges for the same. Motivation in the work environment is just as important as it is outside the work environment. It gives one purpose and the desire to do the best one can possibly do in anything they set out to do.

Reliable

I learned in the Marines and in past work that it is always important to work a full day, regardless of how the day starts or how the day could potentially end. It is entirely too easy to call it quits when things veer off schedule or don't go completely as planned. But the satisfaction felt at the end of a crappy day knowing you stuck it out, clocked out at the end of your shift, and have the opportunity to write over tomorrow is far more important than the instant gratification that comes from immediately exiting a bad situation. Whether it is your fault or someone else's always work a full day. You can quit when the day is over if you like but always finish.

Happy

Anyone that knows me knows that I am a firm believer in the mindset that happiness and joy is a choice, not a feeling or a random happening. That doesn't mean I always get it right but I like to think I do my best. A happy mood can turn a bad day into a good one. It increases productivity in yourself and in others. It also increases the desire to be productive when you haven't been. It's hard to be happy and not want to get stuff done.
